bClick

The ultimate B clicking game. Click B's to get points and unlock better B's. Deposit points into the bank to earn interest and add to your points every second. It's fun. It's addicting. It's bClick!

bOcracy

A sequel of bClick which insists of fighting against B's and A's! Click your side: heal! Click your opponent: attack! "B" the first person to win!!! bOcracy!!

Delta³

A Ludum Dare 35 game based on shape shifting. Type the requested number to advance through the world of polygons, shapes, sides, vertices, and most of all. . . Shape shifting! Shift shapes now!
Specifications: Keyboard needed, works best with chrome.

Googol Mod

Googol Modifier, a Chrome extension used to change Google's logo and many other website's logo by specifying what image you want it to change to! Change the image to anything you want! Promote your company, see your family when you can't, remind you about something, show a meme, or anything else! Modifying logos! Googol Modifier!

RU Cubing

RU Cubing is a Rubik's cube timer for Android, Web, Chrome, and Android Wear. You can time yourself cubing and it also displays a random algorithm to mix up the cube before solving. It has a hold down delay so you can hold down for as long as you want so you don't have to start right when you tap the timer. You can get it for watches too. Made by Cameron Samuels, and android app made together with Jacob Gelder.

Dice Thrower

Have you ever lost your dice while trying to play a board game, or just needed a dice for something like a game or contest? If so, this app will get rid of that need and give it to you! Roll a dice with cool images, sounds, background colors, and also do it right in your Chrome browser on any website with the extension. Click or tap the dice to shake. Also press the spacebar or shake your phone to roll it too! Enjoy throwing! Dice Throw

Moving Mango

Click a moving mango to get a point. Sounds simple. But miss the mango? Then you lose 25 points! See if you can keep up with the speed and madness of Moving Mango!

BNoodle

Need Rubik's cube scrambles when using a SpeedStacks timer or other timer without a scramble? Then you found your solution. Try BNoodle, the Rubik's cube scrambler app to generate up to 3 scrambles at a time. Shake or tap to generate more!

Doodle Dare

Doodle Dare: a game consisting of tilting your phone to stay inside the boundaries for the longest time. See who can stay inside the longest!!

bClick Create

Create your own clicker game just like bClick! Add custom images to click and set your own worth and cost values. You can also convert this into your own app with a unique icon!

Word Maker

For any odd reason: Make your own word. There is not reason to do this, but for fun! Click the word to generate a 5 part word: consonants, vowels, consonants, vowels, then consonants. And best of all, your speaker says the word out loud for pronounciation. Practice with your litte child to let them practice reading! Make words with Word Maker!
